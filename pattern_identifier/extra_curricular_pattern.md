# 6. Extra Curricular Pattern

## Goal
Extract clearly published **extra curricular signals** relevant to parents.

## Rules

1. Prioritize explicit sections titled `Extra Curricular`, `Beyond Academics`, `Student Clubs`, `Activities`, `Sports`, `Arts`, or `Co-Curricular`.
2. Treat mentions of `sports facilities`, `playground`, `athletics`, or `games` as strong sports evidence.
3. Treat mentions of `club`, `student club`, `activity`, `society`, or `house system` as club/activity evidence.
4. Treat mentions of `music`, `dance`, `art`, `drama`, or `cultural` as arts/cultural evidence.
5. Use homepage highlights and dedicated activity pages before inferred generic statements.
6. Ignore ad slogans unless paired with a concrete activity/facility keyword.
7. Prefer evidence from page headings + adjacent content blocks.

## Confidence Guidance

- **High**: Dedicated extra curricular section with specific activities.
- **Medium**: Activity/sports mentions in infrastructure or highlights sections.
- **Low**: Single generic mention without concrete activity words.
